Getting Started

Welcome new adventurers! Here are some tips to get you started on your journey:

  • Understand the Basics: Learn the basic rules and mechanics of the game.
  • Create Your Character: Use the Player's Handbook to create your character.
  • Join a Group: Find a group of players or join an existing campaign.
  • Have Fun: Remember, the most important part of D&D is to have fun!
