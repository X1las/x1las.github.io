5th Edition Items

Items are objects that characters can carry and use in the game. Here are some popular items from D&D 5e:

Bag of Holding

This bag has an interior space considerably larger than its outside dimensions, roughly 2 feet in diameter at the mouth and 4 feet deep. The bag can hold up to 500 pounds, not exceeding a volume of 64 cubic feet. The bag weighs 15 pounds, regardless of its contents.

Ring of Protection

This ring grants a +1 bonus to AC and saving throws while wearing it.

Wand of Magic Missiles

This wand has 7 charges. While holding it, you can use an action to expend 1 or more of its charges to cast the magic missile spell from it. For 1 charge, you cast the 1st-level version of the spell. You can increase the spell slot level by one for each additional charge you expend.

Boots of Elvenkind

While you wear these boots, your steps make no sound, regardless of the surface you are moving across. You also have advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks that rely on moving silently.

Amulet of Health

Your Constitution score is 19 while you wear this amulet. It has no effect on you if your Constitution is already 19 or higher.

Ring of Feather Falling

This ring has 3 charges. While wearing it, you can expend 1 charge as a reaction to falling to slow your descent so that you take no damage from the fall. The ring regains 1d3 expended charges daily at dawn.

Wand of Fireballs

This wand has 7 charges. While holding it, you can use an action to expend 1 or more of its charges to cast the fireball spell from it. For 1 charge, you cast the 3rd-level version of the spell. You can increase the spell slot level by one for each additional charge you expend.
